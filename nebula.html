In the depths of the green nebulae

During the last moments of Nagata's final descent, there was the silver pelican's cry,
that set off a chain reaction of the formation of the Seven Stars of Pattern 6.
The noise that spewed forth from it's clouds were caught in the tangles of shrieking metal and tearing glass.
Brasiall the kill strong, known also as Tazur, was in pursuit to collect the beginnings.
As was Ximitrous the exultant wind catcher, holder of the last Amethyst receiver and heir to his fair Mother's Crown. Thus starting the race that would define the second future...
